# Metric definitions + risk thresholds

Suggested defaults (tune later):

## Ceiling pressure
- High: % Above Max >= 12% OR Offers > Max >= 10%
- Medium: % Above Max 6–11% OR Offers > Max 4–9%
- Low: below that

## Midpoint pressure
- High: % Above Midpoint >= 75% OR median compa >= 1.12
- Medium: 60–74% OR 1.06–1.11
- Low: below that

## Progression drift risk
- High: avg time in level <= 10 months AND promo rate >= 35%
- Medium: avg time 11–14 months OR promo 25–34%
- Low: below that
